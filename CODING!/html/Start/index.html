House blend coffee is a carefully crafted mix of beans, designed to offer a balanced and approachable flavor profile. Typically featuring a combination of beans from different regions, it provides a harmonious blend of rich, smooth, and slightly fruity or nutty notes. French Roast

French Roast coffee is known for its bold, robust flavor and deep, dark color, which comes from the beans being roasted to a rich, almost charcoal-like finish. This roasting process imparts a smoky, slightly bitter taste with hints of caramelized sweetness, making it a favorite among those who enjoy a strong, intense cup of coffee. The beans used in French Roast are typically Arabica, and the dark roast helps to bring out the full-bodied richness that defines this classic coffee style.

Colombian Roast

Colombian Roast coffee is celebrated for its smooth, well-balanced flavor and rich aroma. Sourced from the high-altitude regions of Colombia, these beans are roasted to a medium-dark level, which enhances their natural notes of caramel, chocolate, and a hint of nuttiness. This roast profile brings out a full-bodied taste with a bright acidity and a pleasant, lingering finish. Colombian Roast offers a harmonious blend of robustness and subtlety, making it a popular choice for those who appreciate a flavorful yet approachable cup of coffee.
